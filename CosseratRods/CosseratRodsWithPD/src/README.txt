Run/input:
	main() is in CosseratRodsPD.cpp
	input parameters to be tweaked in input.h
		- enable/disable attachments at endpoints through the 'attachment' variable to see a rod falling or attached at one or two endpoints.

Change view:
	press space key to pause/init simulation
	press esc to exit simulation
	zoom: click left button mouse + move mouse around
	rotate: ctrl pressed while + click left button mouse + move mouse around
	translate: shift pressed while + click left button mouse + move mouse around
